Parallax Scrolling Website: A Visual Delight

A parallax scrolling website is one where the background image moves at a different speed than the foreground content as you scroll down the page. This creates a 3D-like effect, making the website feel more dynamic and engaging.

How it works:

  • Multiple layers: The website is composed of several layers, each moving at a different speed.
  • Scrolling illusion: As you scroll, the layers move at different rates, creating the illusion of depth

Why use it?

  • Enhanced user experience: It offers a visually appealing and interactive experience.
  • Storytelling: It can be used to tell a story or convey information in a creative way.
  • Brand differentiation: It can help your website stand out from the crowd.
